<objective>
Delete the three steps in `.github/workflows/ci.yml` that push `:latest` and `:sha-<7char>` tags to GHCR on every push to main. These steps are the **active** root cause of the `:latest` tag divergence (not a historical artifact per ROADMAP wording) — every merge to main currently overwrites `:latest` via `just image-push` which is a full `docker buildx build --push`, contradicting the D-10 invariant that `:latest` only moves on non-rc stable tag pushes.

Purpose: Without this deletion, the `docker buildx imagetools create` retag in Plan 04 is undone by the very next PR merge. This plan is the prerequisite — everything else in Phase 12.1 stands on this change landing first.

Output: `.github/workflows/ci.yml` with lines 112-131 removed and replaced by a short comment pointing at `main-build.yml` (the new owner of the `:main` tag, created in Plan 02). The `image` job remains as PR-only multi-arch build validation; on push to main it becomes a no-op by design.
</objective>

| push-to-main → ci.yml image job | Currently a CI-credentialed push path to GHCR that writes `:latest` + `:sha-*`. This plan DELETES that boundary (image job becomes read-only on main). |

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
