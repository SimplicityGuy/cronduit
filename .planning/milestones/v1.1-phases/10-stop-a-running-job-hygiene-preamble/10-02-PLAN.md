rand 0.8 → 0.9 API renames:
- `rand::thread_rng()`      → `rand::rng()`        (module-level CSPRNG accessor)
- `rng.gen_range(lo..=hi)`  → `rng.random_range(lo..=hi)`
- `rng.gen::<T>()`          → `rng.random::<T>()`
- `RngCore::fill_bytes(&mut [u8])` → `Rng::fill(&mut [T])`  (the `fill` method now lives on `Rng`, not `RngCore`)
- `SeedableRng` + `StdRng` paths are UNCHANGED in 0.9
