<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| active_runs lock boundary | `Arc<RwLock<HashMap<i64, RunEntry>>>` is shared between scheduler loop (reader+writer), executor (writer on insert/remove), SSE handler (reader). `tokio::sync::RwLock` is fair — writer starvation is impossible, but reader starvation is possible if the writer queue grows. The invariant "lock held only across a single synchronous op" prevents both pathologies. |
| RunEntry ownership boundary | Clones of `broadcast_tx` and `control` live in the map + on the executor's stack. Refcount arithmetic must balance: insert ONE clone at run.rs:102, drop ONE clone at run.rs:277 after remove at run.rs:276. |

|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-10-04-01 | Denial of Service | SSE handler deadlock via read-lock-across-await | mitigate | Pitfall 2 (10-RESEARCH.md).
